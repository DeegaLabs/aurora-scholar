# Naming Rationale - Aurora Scholar

> Rationale and meaning of the project name

## Chosen Name

**Aurora Scholar**

## Meaning and Connection with Purpose

### Aurora

**Meanings:**
- **Clarity**: Aurora represents dawn, the light that brings clarity
- **Authenticity**: Aurora is a natural phenomenon, genuine, cannot be falsified
- **Origin of light**: Symbolizes the origin of knowledge, intellectual provenance
- **New beginning**: Represents a new era in academic publishing

**Connection with project:**
- Brings clarity about the authenticity of academic texts
- Represents the transparency that blockchain provides
- Symbolizes the dawn of a new way to publish knowledge

### Scholar

**Meanings:**
- **Academic**: Directly connects to academic and scientific environment
- **Scholar**: Represents students, researchers, teachers
- **Knowledge**: Evokes learning, research, intellectual production
- **Tradition**: Maintains connection with academic traditions

**Connection with project:**
- 100% academic focus (not generic)
- Clearly identifies target audience
- Conveys seriousness and educational purpose

## Combination: Aurora Scholar

**Why it works:**
- **Short and memorable**: Easy to remember and pronounce
- **Professional**: Sounds serious and academic
- **Distinctive**: Doesn't sound too generic or corporate
- **International**: Works well in Portuguese and English
- **Easy to present**: Ideal for pitch and presentations

## Alternatives Considered

### VeritasChain
- **Pros**: Veritas = truth in Latin, strong connection with authenticity
- **Cons**: May sound too technical, less welcoming

### OnChain Scholar
- **Pros**: Direct, makes blockchain use clear
- **Cons**: Too explicit, may sound limited

### Atena Academic Chain
- **Pros**: Atena = goddess of wisdom, strong symbolism
- **Cons**: Longer, may be difficult to pronounce

### HumanProof
- **Pros**: Direct about the problem (proving humanity)
- **Cons**: May sound negative, focuses on problem not solution

### AuthenText
- **Pros**: Interesting wordplay
- **Cons**: May sound too commercial, less academic

### Provenance Scholar
- **Pros**: Provenance = origin, central concept
- **Cons**: Less known word, may confuse

## Suggested Tagline

### Options

1. **"Academic Authorship & On-Chain Publishing"**
   - Descriptive and clear
   - Good for official documentation

2. **"Authentic, Verifiable and Permanent Knowledge"**
   - Focus on benefits
   - In English for international reach

3. **"The Future of Academic Publishing"**
   - Future vision
   - In English for international reach

4. **"Academic Authenticity on Blockchain"**
   - Direct and clear
   - Focuses on differentiator

### Recommendation

**For hackathon:** "Academic Authorship & On-Chain Publishing"

**For marketing:** "Authentic, Verifiable and Permanent Knowledge"

## Name Usage

### Formal Contexts
- Technical documentation
- Academic presentations
- Official submissions
- **Format:** "Aurora Scholar"

### Informal Contexts
- Communication with developers
- Online communities
- **Format:** "Aurora Scholar" or "aurora-scholar" (kebab-case)

### Code and Repositories
- **GitHub:** `aurora-scholar` (kebab-case)
- **Package names:** `@aurora-scholar/*` (scoped packages)
- **Domain:** `aurora-scholar.io` or `aurorascholar.org`

## Brand Considerations

### Brand Personality
- **Serious but accessible**: Academic without being elitist
- **Innovative but reliable**: Blockchain without being too experimental
- **Ethical and transparent**: Core project values

### Target Audience
- University students
- Researchers
- Teachers and advisors
- Academic institutions

### Positioning
- **Is not**: A generic writing tool
- **Is**: A specialized academic platform
- **Differentiator**: Verifiable authenticity + ethical AI
